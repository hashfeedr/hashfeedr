About HashFeedr

HashFeedr is a real-time Twitter tracker designed to run on a big screen (e.g. a beamer) to provide the necessary 'twentertainment' at your next party. Simple decide on an hashtag an run Hashfeedr on your big motherf-ing screen.

HashFeedr uses Django, Twisted, Redis, HTML5 WebSockets and of course Twitters excellent streaming API to provide you with tweets in mere miliseconds. Polling is for losers.
